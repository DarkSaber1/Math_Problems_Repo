# Task 3

The monthly cost is

$$
U = 15000X + 10000\sqrt{X}
$$

where the number of employees $X$ has distribution:

| $x$ | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $P(X=x)$ | 0.10 | 0.25 | 0.40 | 0.25 |

---

## 1. Calculate the cost for each value of $X$

For $X=2$:

$$
U = 15000(2) + 10000\sqrt{2}
= 30000 + 14142.14
\approx 44142.14
$$

For $X=3$:

$$
U = 15000(3) + 10000\sqrt{3}
= 45000 + 17320.51
\approx 62320.51
$$

For $X=4$:

$$
U = 15000(4) + 10000\sqrt{4}
= 60000 + 20000
= 80000
$$

For $X=5$:

$$
U = 15000(5) + 10000\sqrt{5}
= 75000 + 22360.68
\approx 97360.68
$$

---

## 2. Distribution of $U$

Each cost value comes from one value of $X$, so the probabilities stay the same.

| $u$ | 44142.14 | 62320.51 | 80000 | 97360.68 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| $P(U=u)$ | 0.10 | 0.25 | 0.40 | 0.25 |

---

## Final Answer

The probability function of the random variable $U$ is:

$$
P(U=44142.14)=0.10
$$

$$
P(U=62320.51)=0.25
$$

$$
P(U=80000)=0.40
$$

$$
P(U=97360.68)=0.25
$$
